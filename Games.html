Medieval Land

The game offers players the ability to manage their own farm, grow plants, take care of animals, explore, and other interactions. It features a strong focus on building 3D structures, allowing players to build their own house, barns, among others. In addition, the game also has a crafting system where players can collect resources and use them to craft useful items such as tools and other equipment.

Atenas

Athens is a 2D game that explores themes of psychological illness through an emotional journey. The player controls a character lost in a mysterious tower, representing mental darkness. Facing challenges that personify psychological disorders, the protagonist regains mental clarity, returning to the beginning of the tower and unraveling the mysteries that surround it.

Karawara

The game will be a Action RPG based on the indigenous-Brazilian culture, that is, it encompasses from the visual characteristics of the aspects of religious beliefs and myths. Therefore, the project promises to deliver more than a moment of entertainment. We want players to learn more about the rich aspects of the country's roots. Therefore, regardless of the position that the player Occupy, this will be a place for fun and learning.
